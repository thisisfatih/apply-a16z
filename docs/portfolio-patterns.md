## SR006 Dominant Thesis: "AI Workforce for X"

The clearest template that won in SR006: pick a regulated, paper-heavy vertical → automate it with AI agents.

**Verticals that clustered:**
- Financial services (Bilrost/loans, August/banking, Third Space/insurance, Margin/credit)
- Legal/compliance (Concorda/lawyers, ZeroDrift/compliance)
- Construction (Cascade, Piper-ai)
- Tax (Grove Tax, Taxnova, Quanto/accounting)

## Team Size at Application

SR006 median: 4 people. Multiple 2-person teams accepted with outsized prior track records.

Most accepted teams: 2–3 founders with directly complementary skills.

Examples of strong cofounder combinations:
- Lawyer (14yr gaming privacy) + CS engineer (Concorda)
- Ex-Snap Sr. Dir. Engineering + Stanford MBA matchmaker (Sitch)
- VALORANT designer + Freckle co-founder (Double Dusk)
