---
name: research
description: Deep-dive into a founder's background and market to surface earned secrets and unfair advantages before running /apply. Use this when a founder isn't sure what makes them unique or wants to do prep work before the full application pipeline.
---

Run the `earned-secrets` skill in standalone mode as a research sprint.

Collect from founder:
- Names of all founders and their backgrounds
- Company name, domain, and any existing work (URL, repo, demo)
- How long they've been in this market (as operator, user, or builder)

Then run the full five-round Socratic interview from the `earned-secrets` skill.

Output a Founder Research Brief:
```
FOUNDER RESEARCH BRIEF — [Company]

EARNED SECRET
[3-sentence statement]

EVIDENCE BASE
[bullet list with source types]

COMPETITIVE MOAT
[1 sentence]

GAPS TO FILL BEFORE /APPLY
[list of missing intake data]
```

This brief feeds directly into `/apply`. Tell the founder to run `/apply` when ready and this context will carry over.
